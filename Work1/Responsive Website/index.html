About

BMW M5:

Very few sedans generate as much heart beating and adrenaline as the BMW M5. Especially when it comes in a limited Special Edition with Competition Package. Cast in the visually stunning BMW Individual special paint finish: Mineral White metallic, this exceptional BMW M5 also sets the pulses racing with its elegant side. The specially adjusted ride height lowered by 10 millimetres provides more stability, both on the race track and in everyday use. `

Whats in it?

In this Special Edition, the performance of the powerful 4.4 litre M TwinPower Turbo 8-cylinder petrol engine has been increased to a breathtaking 600 HP (441 kW). The steering with M specific Servotronic Mode and directly configured characteristic curve ensure that every steering command is performed with maximum precision.

ENGINE:

4.4 litre M TwinPower Turbo 8-cylinder petrol engine.

Performance increase to 441 kW/600 HP.

Modified exhaust system with distinctly sporty motor sound.


DRIVING PERFORMANCE:

From 0 to 100 km/h in 3.9 seconds (with M DCT)

From 0 to 200 km/h in 11.9 seconds (with M DCT)

Acceleration from 80 to 120 km/h in fourth/fifth gear in 3.6/4.5 seconds (with M DCT)
